Overview

InsightEye is an innovative tool designed to assess mental health through real-time eye-tracking analysis, leveraging Mediapipe for facial landmark detection and Generative AI for data interpretation. This system aims to provide non-invasive, cost-effective, and quick insights into mental and neurological health by analyzing key eye movement metrics.

Technologies Used

  • Mediapipe: For pose detection and movement analysis.
  • Django: Backend framework for processing and data handling.
  • Bootstrap: Frontend framework for creating a visually appealing interface.

Key Features

  • Blink Rate Monitoring: Tracks and calculates the frequency of eye blinks, providing indicators of stress, fatigue, or cognitive workload.
  • Gaze Stability Analysis: Measures the steadiness of the gaze, detecting possible signs of anxiety, distraction, or neurological disorders.
  • Saccadic Speed Evaluation: Analyzes rapid eye movements to detect irregularities that may indicate fatigue, stress, or neurocognitive conditions.
  • Generative AI Insights: Summarizes eye-tracking data into actionable mental health insights, enhancing user understanding and decision-making.

Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR)

The Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) is a metric used to measure eye openness. It is calculated using specific eye landmarks detected by Mediapipe. The EAR value decreases when the eyes are closed, enabling blink detection and drowsiness monitoring.
